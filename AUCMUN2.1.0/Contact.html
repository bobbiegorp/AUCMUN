When and how can I register a delegation to attend AUCMUN 2018?
You can register for delegate here and for chairs here.

What happens after I register?
Following registration, the AUCMUN Secretariat will review your application and either approve or decline it. Please note rejections are rare, but may become more frequent as capacity is neared. As we maintain beginner, intermediate and advanced committees, we cater for a wide variety of experience with Model United Nations and debate.

How do I make payment for the conference?
More information on this will follow soon

Can I attend AUCMUN alone or do I have to be part of a larger delegation?
You can register as an independent delegate.

I have never participated in Model United Nations before. Can I still attend the conference?
Committees at AUCMUN are divided into beginner, intermediate, and advanced. For this reason you are welcome to attend even if you have little or no experience.

How do I prepare for the conference?
Once allocated a country to represent and a committees to debate in, you will have ample time to familiarise yourself with the topics of discussion and the ways of Model United Nations.
Study guides for the topics debated in specific committee will be made available. AUCMUN also has number of more general help guides to assist delegates in their preparation. For this please see here.

Can secondary school students attend AUCMUN?
AUCMUN is primarily a university-level conference. We do however accept a limited number of students from secondary schools to aid them develop more rigorous thinking. These students need to have MUN experience though.
