The Teams


Secretariat

x

Let us introduce you to the executive secretariat!
Our job is to ensure a smooth and structured functioning for you throughout the conference in terms of execution and management. We are in charge of organising the content of the conference; we have selected the topics of discussion in your committees, we have allocated the delegates and will ensure that all the information about the programme, venues and activities are provided to you.

The chair manager of this conference, Calvin, will be recruiting chairs, training them for the conference in April, and revising the Chair guides that will be provided to our delegates.
The material manager, Nelly, has designed our logo, written the brochures that will be delivered to you during our conference and is in charge of all your placards, posters and information flyers.
The topic manager, Shambhavi has created the agenda for the conference, and has established the topics that will be discussed in the Human Rights, Special Political, ECOSOC and Security Council Committees.
The Deputy Secretary General, Ruth, is in charge of administrating and supervising the work of the members of the executive secretariat team and helps them carry out their functions.

Logistics

x
The logistics team has worked hard on ensuring that the 2017 AUCMUN Conference goes smoothly. Working together: Floortje, Milena, Renske, and Klaudia, are here to guarantee our delegates and chairs housing, travelling information, state-of-the-art conference venue, and the best social program the city has to offer. Our utmost priority is to make delegates feel welcomed and well-informed about all events. Having previously participated in the MUN conferences, the logistics team takes into account possible inconveniences. Please do not hesitate to contact us in case you have any questions. We will be more than happy to provide you with answers.

PR

x
Hello everyone! Let us introduce; our Public Relations Team!
The aim of the PR team is to spread the word about AUCMUN and to attract a group of enthusiastic delegates. This is through actively reaching out to other schools, but also create an online platform for aspiring participants to find us.

Rachelle as Head of PR keeps overview over all of the tasks of the team, keeps track of time and deadlines and her main goal is to inspire as many students as possible to join the AUCMUN event, through coordinating the other PR tasks and reaching out to other universities.
Katia is the Applications Manager, she is the one responsible for a smooth and reliable applications procedure. The event really starts from the moment students apply, so designing and keeping track of applications is very important.
Our Marketing Manager is Giovanni, he is the one to actively reach out to students, within AUC but especially outside the bubble, to inform them about AUCMUN and to get them to join and participate in this great event. He will be doing so by sending invitations out to other universities and former participants, and also by doing presentations all over the Netherlands.
Then, we have a Press Manager, Femke, who is in charge of the Facebook promotion and all tasks surrounding the social media promotion. One of these tasks is taking photos of the event, and updating on preparations.
Last but most certainly not least, there’s Bobbie, our Website Manager. His job is to coordinate the website: http://aucmun.nl/. This means creating a website and keeping it up-to-date, harmonized with the Facebook page, so people will be able to find us online and to keep track of important developments and information. The Press Team is very excited for the conference, and will keep you informed on what you need to know! Hope to see you 8th and 9th of April!

Finances

x
The Finance Team consists of 4 members:
Head of Finances - Ana Chaloska
Finance Manager - Tijana Istochka
Sponsor Manager - Juanita Guttiérrez
Sponsor Manager - Pieter Weijerman

The Finance Team of AUCMUN 2017 keeps track of the funds needed to organize the conference through communication with the other teams regarding the budget and maintains the availability of the funds through making and updating a finances overview. Besides, the team focusses on getting sponsors for the conference to supply additionally needed resources and financial support to make it a successful event. It does this through sending emails to and meeting with possible institutions/companies that might be interested in funding the event.
