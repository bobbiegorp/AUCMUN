WELCOME TO AUCMUN 2017


Distinguished delegates and dear friends of AUCMUN,

Welcome to Amsterdam University College Model United Nations 2017!

This year we are hosting the 2nd edition of the conference, during the second weekend of April - Saturday the 8th and Sunday the 9th. The conference will once more take place at Amsterdam University College, in the east of Amsterdam, not too far from the center.

As an MUN-addict I am proud to present you this conference, I have had the privilege to attend many conferences and I have realized how magnificent it is to take part. Therefore being this year's Secretary-General makes me even more honoured. We want you to experience a likewise event, of high quality, and so we encourage you all to take part.

Based on last years conference, and the increasing interest for MUN at our University, we are now expanding the conference, and this year, will host four committees and we are increasing the conference in big numbers. That said, we retain our commitment to high quality debates with smooth conference mechanics behind the scenes, and hope to deliver a great AUCMUN!

We hope to see you in April!

Sincerely,
Ishvar Lalbahadoersing

Secretary-General
Amsterdam University College Model United Nations 2017
